Part I: Planning & Design (15 pts)

* Decide on a game idea and core goal
* Game rules and player actions are written or sketched out
* Main objects (player, obstacles, goals, enemies, etc.) are identified
* Sketch a simple plan showing how the game will work step-by-step
* Identify which icons you are going to use and get the unicode for those icons
* Determine your board size
* Determine your scoring system
* Determine win / lose conditions
* Outline expansion ideas

Game Idea:
Horror personality quiz

Game Rules:
Three options, must pick one of the options 
Answer honestly 
Finish the game 

Set-up:
Set characters/personality type presets 
5 questions, they get scarier as they go on 
3 different personality types 
Reach the end of the game and get a personality type 

Major Game Elements:
Main character that makes choices
Goal: reach the end of the game and get a "personality" 
Icons and information appear when the player gets to the end

Gameplay Process:
Picking answers that align with what you feel
Getting through all the answers
Getting a personality 

Board size: 10
Icons: When getting different personalities get different character icons and pictures that show up with the question
Scoring system: You win by finishing the game, each option goes towards a different ending

1F6BD(dysentary)
1F3A2(final destination)
